\postepi{

\emph{Then, they say, for the sole time in these parts,}\\
\emph{White cows could not be found for Juno's rites,}\\
\emph{And buffaloes, instead, in ill-matched pairs,}\\
\emph{Drew chariots to the lofty treasure-house.}\\
\emph{Men hacked the ground with rakes, and dug a seed}\\
\emph{With blistered fingers, hitched themselves to carts}\\
\emph{And, straining forward, dragged the creaking loads.}\\
\emph{Wolves ceased to prowl at night around the fold,}\\
\emph{For sharper worries preyed upon their minds;}\\
\emph{The timid roe and flying stag made friends}\\
\emph{With hounds and wandered all around the farms.}\\
\emph{The brood of the mighty sea was washed ashore}\\
\emph{Like flotsam from a shipwreck, and the seals}\\
\emph{Escaped to unfamiliar inland streams.}\\
\emph{The viper died defenseless in her lair}\\
\emph{And water snakes, their scales erect in fear.}\\
\emph{Birds found the air too heavy for their wings}\\
\emph{And, plunging earthward, left their lives aloft.}\\
\emph{Change of pasture made no difference}\\
\emph{And remedies effected only harm;}\\
\emph{The masters of the healing arts gave up.}\\
\emph{Pale Fury, flown from Hell's depths to the light,}\\
\emph{Tisiphone, drove on Disease and Fear,}\\
\emph{Her greedy head rose higher every day.}\\
\emph{The bleating of the herds, their steady moans,}\\
\emph{Filled all the river beds and thirsty banks,}\\
\emph{Reverberating through the supine hills.}\\
\emph{The Fury dealt out multitudinous deaths,}\\
\emph{Heaving up cadavers in the stalls,}\\
\emph{Rotting corpses stank and putrefied,}\\
\emph{Until at last men buried them in pits.}\\
\emph{The hides were useless, and the flesh so foul}\\
\emph{That fire and water could not salvage it;}\\
\emph{None could shear the fleece, nor touch the web.}\\
\emph{If someone tried to wear the loathsome cloth,}\\
\emph{He burned and blistered, rank repulsive sweat}\\
\emph{Poured off his fetid limbs; and, soon enough,}\\
\emph{His stricken body felt the sacred fire.}\\

\qsource{Virgil}
