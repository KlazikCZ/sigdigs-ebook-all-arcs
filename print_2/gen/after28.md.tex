\emph{This is the Way for men who want to learn my strategy:}\\
\emph{First, do not think dishonestly.}\\
\emph{Second, the Way is in training.}\\
\emph{Third, become acquainted with every art.}\\
\emph{Fourth, know the Ways of all professions.}\\
\emph{Fifth, distinguish between gain and loss in worldly matters.}\\
\emph{Sixth, develop intuitive judgment and understanding for
everything.}\\
\emph{Seventh, perceive those things which cannot be seen.}\\
\emph{Eighth, pay attention even to trifles.}\\
\emph{Ninth, do nothing which is of no use.}\\
\bigskip
\qsource{\emph{The Book of Five Rings}, Miyamoto Musashi}
